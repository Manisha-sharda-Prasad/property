# Ghaziabad Plot Purchase – Complete Due Diligence & POA Guide

## 1. Ownership & Title Verification (MOST CRITICAL)

| # | Checkpoint | What to Verify | Notes / Risk |
|---|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| 1 | **Freehold Title** | Property must be **freehold**, not leasehold | Safest ownership type |
| 2 | **Sale Deed / Mother Deed** | Continuous ownership chain (15–20 yrs) till mother deed | Lawyer must verify disputes |
| 3 | **Encumbrance Certificate (EC)** | EC from Sub‑Registrar (15–20 yrs) | Must show sale, mortgage & loan closure |
| 4 | **Bank Loan Closure** | Bank NOC, loan closure cert, title release letter | Must match EC entries |
| 5 | **Inheritance Proof (if applicable)** | Death cert, legal heir cert, succession deed, probate (if will) | ❌ Missing docs = high risk |
| 6 | **Seller Identity** | Original Aadhaar + PAN | Mandatory for registry & TDS |
| 7 | **Marital Status** | Spouse consent if married / ancestral | Common UP practice |

## 2. Land Use, Layout & Authority Approvals

| # | Checkpoint | What to Verify | Notes |
|---|-----------|---------------|------|
| 8 | **Land Use / CLU** | Agricultural → Residential conversion (if applicable) | Cross‑check revenue records |
| 9 | **Approved Layout Plan** | GDA / Authority approved layout | ❌ No hand‑drawn / proposed plans |
| 10 | **Plot Matching** | Plot number, area, road access match layout | Avoid mismatch |
| 11 | **Authority NOC** | GDA / Nagar Nigam / Awas Vikas | No dues / no violations |
| 12 | **Non‑Acquisition Cert (Optional)** | Plot not under govt acquisition | Good to have |
| 13 | **Road Access Proof** | Approved access road in layout | Avoid land‑locked plots |

## 7. ABSOLUTE RED FLAGS (STOP IMMEDIATELY)

- Seller provides **only photocopies**
- EC shows unexplained mortgage entries
- Plot not in **approved layout**
- No **CLU** for residential land
- Seller avoids mutation
- No bank NOC despite loan history
- Boundary mismatch with layout / FMB

---

## 8. First Questions to Ask the Seller (Short & Practical)

1. Is the plot **freehold**?
2. Are you the **sole owner**? Any co‑owners/heirs?
3. Can you show **original sale deed chain (15–20 yrs)**?
4. Any loan or mortgage ever? Bank NOC?
5. Is land use **residential** (approved layout / CLU)?
6. Is mutation done in your name?
7. Any dues pending (tax, society, GDA)?
8. Any dispute, notice, or court case?
9. Is road access approved?
10. Why are you selling?

## 9. Transaction Flow (Before → During → After Registration)

### A. Before Registration

| Step | Action | Key Notes |
|-----|-------|----------|
| 1 | Token / Booking (1–5%) | NEFT/RTGS/Cheque + receipt |
| 2 | Due Diligence | No major payment |
| 3 | Funds Prep | Sale amount + stamp + registry |

### B. Registration Day

| Step | Action | Key Notes |
|-----|-------|----------|
| 4 | Sale Deed Execution | At Sub‑Registrar |
| 5 | Final Payment | RTGS / DD at registrar |
| 6 | Registration Complete | Biometrics + registration |

### C. After Registration

| Step | Action | Key Notes |
|-----|-------|----------|
| 7 | Possession Handover | Originals + keys |
| 8 | Mutation & Tax Transfer | Buyer applies |

**Golden Rule:**
- ❌ Never pay full amount before registry
- ✅ Pay bulk amount only on registry day
- ✅ Use traceable banking channels

## 10. Power of Attorney (POA) – NRI Process (USA → UP)

### Steps Summary

1. Indian lawyer drafts POA → sends PDF
2. Print 2 originals on plain paper
3. Sign before US Notary (with passport)
4. Apply via **CGI–San Francisco / VFS**
5. Mail complete packet (notarized POA + docs)
6. Receive attested POA
7. Courier original to India
8. Stamp/adjudicate in UP (₹100–₹500)

### Typical Documents Required

- Original notarized POA
- CGI‑SF application form
- Passport notarized copy
- Visa / I‑797 / GC notarized copy
- US address proof notarized
- VFS payment receipt
- Prepaid return envelope
